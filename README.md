## About

KeenKeeper is a personal relationship management app built to help you stay intentionally connected with the people who matter most. Life gets busy — friendships fade not from indifference, but from neglect. KeenKeeper solves that by letting you set check-in goals for each friend, track your interactions, and get a clear picture of where your attention is going.

No social feed. No noise. Just your people, and a gentle reminder to show up for them.

---

## Technologies Used

React, React Router, Tailwind CSS, DaisyUI, Recharts, React Hot Toast, React Icons, Context API

---

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. Smart Friend Dashboard
Every friend card shows their current connection status — **On Track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue** — calculated from your check-in goal and the days since your last contact. At a glance, you always know who needs your attention most.

### 2. One-Click Check-In Logging
From any friend's detail page, log a **Call**, **Text**, or **Video** interaction in a single click. A toast confirmation appears instantly, and the entry flows live into both the Timeline and Analytics — no page reload, no friction.

### 3. Timeline & Friendship Analytics
The **Timeline** gives you a scrollable, filterable history of every interaction across all your friends. The **Stats** page turns that data into a live donut chart, breaking down how you communicate — so you can see your habits clearly and adjust intentionally.
