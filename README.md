# 🌿 KeenKeeper

> Your personal shelf of meaningful connections. Browse, tend, and nurture the relationships that matter most.

---

## 📖 About

KeenKeeper is a relationship management app that helps you stay intentionally connected with the people who matter most in your life. Set check-in goals for each friend, log interactions, and never let an important relationship quietly fade away.

## ✨ Key Features

### 1. 📋 Smart Friend Dashboard
View all your friends at a glance with their contact status — **On Track**, **Almost Due**, or **Overdue** — so you always know who needs your attention next.

### 2. ⚡ Quick Check-In Logging
From any friend's detail page, log a **Call**, **Text**, or **Video** interaction in one click. Every entry is instantly reflected in the Timeline and Analytics — in real time, no refresh needed.

### 3. 📊 Interaction Analytics & Timeline
The **Timeline** page shows a chronological history of all your interactions with filter support by type. The **Stats** page visualizes your communication habits with a live donut chart — so you can see at a glance whether you're calling, texting, or video-calling your people most.
